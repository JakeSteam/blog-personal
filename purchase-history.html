Google Play purchase history analyser

Load your Purchase History.json below (instructions), or load sample data (mine!).

Notes:

  • No data is transferred, all processing is done in your browser.
  • "Total" is in your local currency, if you've purchased using multiple this tool won't be accurate.
  • Free purchases (e.g. trials) are not included in the analysis.
  • Refunded purchases will still be shown.
